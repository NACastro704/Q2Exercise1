MATCHA

Wilson Khor W.H.



Even if all i gained

from the sun above and the earth below

is nothing

but bitterness,



Even if all Man does

is choking me for flavour

and grind me with his hands

down to the finest powder...



Let my bitterness be umami

and my sould be poetry

as poets trace my every grain and vein

with words that would last centuries



Wilson Khor's Matcha
